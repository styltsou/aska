# Premium Interaction Ideas

These are focused enhancements for Aska's core workflows. They should remain
short, purposeful, and respect reduced-motion preferences.

## Folder Navigation

- Let a folder preview grid briefly inform the destination board transition.
- At minimum, reveal the destination board with a short opacity and position
  transition that preserves the sense of navigation.

## Asset Actions

- Animate deleted cards closed and offer a compact Undo toast.

## Image Viewer

- Fade the backdrop and expand the image from its card position while
  retaining its aspect ratio.

## Note Creation

- Open the editor with focus already in the primary field and reveal the
  editing surface subtly instead of using a generic modal pop-in.

The highest-value next step is richer folder-target feedback during canvas drag
and drop because it improves the primary organization workflow without adding
decorative motion elsewhere.
